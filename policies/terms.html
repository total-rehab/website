Services provided by Total Rehab

Total Rehab provides a mobile app that helps You recover from common lower-limb sports injuries. It does so by providing access to conservative exercise-based rehabilitation plans as well as curated tips & information on how to manage the symptoms of the injury.

Based on the selected rehabilitation plan, You will get access to a structured plan of training sessions including videos and detailed written instructions of each exercise to perform. The App will offer you to increase or reduce the intensity of the training sessions based on Your feedback about perceived effort and whether you experienced any pain.

When to use the app and when not

The Total Rehab App is intended to support the recovery using exercise training from the following common musculoskeletal sports injuries and post-operation surgery:

- Ankle and Foot Strains and Sprains

- Musculoskeletal Shoulder Pathologies

- Hamstring Strains

- Musculoskeletal Lower Back Pathologies

- Total Knee Replacement Surgery

- Total Hip Replacement Surgery

- ACL Rupture Surgery

It should only be used if you have been diagnosed with such a condition by a medical professional. If You are not certain that you have one of the mentioned conditions, please consult a medical professional first before you start using the App.

Total Rehab App does not replace the diagnosis or treatment by a medical professional. If you have suffered a blunt trauma, experience acute pain or your symptoms worsen please seek medical help immediately.
