Meniscus Tear

Information about the program

The Meniscus Tear Rehabilitation Program is designed to support you in addressing and recuperating from meniscus tear pain and issues. This injury can result from multiple factors, including sports-related incidents, wear and tear, or sudden twists and turns. Your full dedication to this program for its entire duration is highly advised. It plays a pivotal role in expediting your healing process and restoring your capacity to partake in regular activities and daily life.

Your progression matters

After each exercise, we will ask you about your pain scale and adapt the program for the following days and weeks to provide a more suitable program for your progression.

Aim of the program

  • Reduce acute swelling immediately after the injury

  • Improve flexibility and mobility of the surrounding areas

  • Functionally strengthening the muscles around your knee, foot, and surrounding muscles

  • Return to sports or day-to-day related activities

Equipment Required

  • Cold Pack

  • Towel

  • Resistance Bands
