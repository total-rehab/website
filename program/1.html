Ankle and Foot

Information about the program

The 10-week Ankle and Foot program is designed to help guide and support you through ankle and foot pain and discomfort. This of which could be caused by sports-related injuries, posture-related issues, or muscular strains. We encourage you to actively follow the program from beginning to end. This program is designed to support your return to physical activity and day-to-day activities.

Your progression matters

We will ask you about your pain scale after each exercise and adapt the program for the following days and weeks to provide a more suitable program for your progression.

Aim of the program

  • Reduce acute swelling immediately after the injury

  • Encourage proprioception (balance)

  • Improve flexibility and mobility of the surrounding areas

  • Functionally strengthening your ankle, foot, and surrounding muscles

  • Improve posture

  • Return to sports or day-to-day related activities

Equipment Required

  • Cold and Heat Pack

  • Ankle Support

  • Towel

  • Resistance Bands
